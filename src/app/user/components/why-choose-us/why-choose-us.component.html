Kashmir
during Winter

During winter, Kashmir transforms into a winter wonderland, draped in a blanket of pristine snow. The Himalayan peaks and Dal Lake freeze, creating a magical landscape. The chilly air resonates with the warmth of Kashmiri hospitality, making it an enchanting destination for those seeking a serene and snowy retreat.

Kashmir
during Summer

In summer, Kashmir blossoms into a vibrant paradise of lush meadows, blooming gardens, and shimmering lakes. The snow-capped mountains provide a picturesque backdrop, inviting visitors to explore its scenic beauty. The pleasant weather makes it an ideal season to experience the rich culture and tranquility that define this breathtaking region.
